How to Play

Choose your difficulty, category, and amount of questions. Then click "Start Game" to begin. You will be presented with a series of multiple choice questions. Select the answer you believe to be correct and then the game will proceed to the next question. You are given two "lifelines" during the game, 50/50 and Phone-a-Friend. 50/50 will take away two incorrect answers and Phone-a-Friend will give you the correct answer. Upon completion of the game you will be presented with your score and the option to play again or see some statistics. Good luck and enjoy some trivia!
